IBM Bluemix Hackathon, Spring 2017


IoT HARDWARE HACKATHON

WED, FEB 22 - THURS, FEB 23

Teams of 2 to 4 students will prototype an internet connected device to solve a given challenge. All teams receive free hardware to prototype and develop their designs, as well as training from IBM engineers on using Bluemix web services for the Internet of Things (IoT).

Cash Prizes for 1st, 2nd and 3rd place.

Location: EE bldg., Room TBD.

Schedule: Teams will do the onsite development 5 p.m. Feb. 22 until 12 p.m. Feb. 23. Dinner, breakfast and lunch will be provided.

Participating hackers will have access to:

  • Electronics Labs

  • Test Equipment

  • 3D Printers

  • PCB Plotters

  • Prototyping Boards

  • and other Hardware.
