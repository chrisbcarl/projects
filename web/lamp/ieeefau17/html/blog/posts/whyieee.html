Why IEEE?

by James Phillips

Posted on March 20, 2015

Short answer: IEEE is awesome!

IEEE has contributed in many ways to the world. One of those ways has been through education. IEEE has an e-learning library archive that harbors educational programs and interactive tutorials. Individuals can learn at their own convenience about the latest worldly knowledge in communications to aerospace to even bioengineering and more. IEEE Educations Partners Program offers individuals the ability to earn a certification after completing a course or online degree. The IEEE continue to renew their archive so everyone can stay up to date with the latest technological trend and information.

Being a member of this prestigious association offers some outstanding benefits for members. As mentioned in the previous paragraph, the association offers a digital library for your convenience. Including but not limited to magazines, journals, e-books and so on. Depending on whether you are a student or not, IEEE provides scholarships and awards to assist you in your endeavors. Members can connect with professionals to expand their opportunity for a future career or develop their career already. There is plenty of room for new members to join to sharpen their skills and experience.
