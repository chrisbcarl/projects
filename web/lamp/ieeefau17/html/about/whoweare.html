Who we are:

IEEE Student Branch at FAU is an organization of dedicated and inspired engineering students who share a common goal. That goal is to grow themselves as professional engineers by growing their knowledge base through classes, bolstering their experience through hands-on training, expanding their network through activity and influencing the community through outreach. Each engineer in IEEE FAU Student Branch has made a strong commitment to help one another grow and develop. Through teamwork and engineering leadership our members will be ready to exceed current standards of engineering excellence and set standards of their own. We desire to brighten the future by fulfilling our potentials and working together to advance the world of technology.


Our mission as an organization:

As a student branch representation of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, our student organization aims to adequately emulate IEEE’s practices and traditions. We share their passion for setting the worlds standards for technological development and research. We hope to be a part of their bright future all the while relishing their vibrant history.


IEEE’s mission statement:

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.


IEEE’s motto and what it means:


-Advancing Technology for Humanity
This motto is neither a goal nor an aspiration but rather a promise that the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers will uphold the standards for development of current technologies so that all of our vast goals as humans are achieved.


Why we do what we do:


As undergraduate, graduate and PhD students in engineering, our objective is to carve out our futures as proficiently as possible so that we can play vital roles in the quest to advance technology. Our engineers will go on to change the world piece by piece and we will get there with our efforts here and now.
