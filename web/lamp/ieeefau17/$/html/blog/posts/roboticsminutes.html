Robotics Club - Meetings Minutes

by Washington Garcia

Posted on June 9, 2014

Each member come up with an interpretation of a top down design that uses one arm for all challenges.

Started at 4:00PM

We discussed the basic hierarchy for the design, the brain is going to be the microcontroller where it stems down to the body. From the body, it splits into mobility and functions. Mobility splits into a start/finish restrictions and navigation; most likely use omni-wheels. Functions are the challenges. While discussing the functions that are needed for the challenge, we worked out the basic design of the robot to be a top-down platform design. We will have an arm with a telescoping action coming down from the platform.
